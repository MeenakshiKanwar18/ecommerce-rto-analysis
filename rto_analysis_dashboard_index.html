🔴 Meesho RTO at 38.7% — Needs Immediate Action
Despite 2,960 orders, Meesho's net effective revenue after RTO costs is only ~₹2.6L — least profitable per order. Nearly all driven by COD. Recommend pre-delivery confirmation calls immediately. If no improvement in 60 days, consider pausing Meesho and reallocating that budget to Amazon sponsored ads.
🟠 COD Orders Cost ₹1.9L Extra Per Period
COD RTO (31.8%) is 6.5× higher than Prepaid (4.9%). Converting just 20% of COD to Prepaid with a ₹30 discount would recover ~₹1.4L in shipping costs per period — paying for the incentive in under 2 weeks.
🟢 Amazon Is the Business Engine — Invest More Here
Amazon: 62% of GMV, 44% margin, 9.2% RTO — best on all metrics. AOV of ₹371 is 2.5× Meesho. Recommendation: Increase Amazon PPC budget by 30%, focus A+ content on top 5 SKUs, target 500+ reviews on hero products to boost organic ranking.
🟢 Top 3 SKUs = 54% of Revenue — Protect These
iPhone 15 cases, Samsung Galaxy cases, and tempered glass bundles drive over half of all revenue with lowest RTO and highest margins. These must have priority in inventory planning, ads, and listing optimization. A stockout on any of these directly hurts the business.
🟡 Personalized Cases = Untapped Margin Opportunity
Personalized cases carry 55% gross margin vs 32–38% for standard cases. Volume is currently low, but the margin is exceptional. A targeted Instagram/Facebook campaign with a ₹5,000 test budget could significantly improve blended margin if ROAS exceeds 4×.
🔵 Feb–Mar Spike = Seasonal Pattern to Exploit
GMV jumped 34% in Feb–Mar, likely Valentine's Day gifting and end-of-quarter spending. Recommendation: Pre-stock inventory by January 15, run early-bird promotions from Jan 20, and create Valentine's Day personalized case bundles for next cycle.
